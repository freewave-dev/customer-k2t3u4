About the company

Built from the ground up in the Skagit Valley.

Dariotis Construction began serving customers in western Washington in 1976. Our nursery and office are located in the Skagit Valley, and our crews work the corridor from Blaine to Bellevue.

Since 1977 we've built more than $14 million of parks, commercial landscapes, and athletic fields. Every project is unique — depending on what each client needs, we pull resources from other fields and partner with other talented businesses across the region.

What we build

A full-service landscape construction partner.

From the first cut of earth to the last shrub planted, our crews handle the work — and the coordination — that keeps projects on schedule and on budget.

Commercial Prevailing Wage

Public works and prevailing wage projects delivered with the documentation and discipline they require.

Earthwork & Grading

Laser-controlled grading and earthwork with our Cat D-3 dozer and Kobelco backhoe — precise from rough cut to finish.

Irrigation Installation

Designed and installed systems that keep fields green, plantings healthy, and water bills in check year-round.

Plant Selection & Installation

Working from our own 18-acre Skagit Valley nursery, we select and install plant material suited to the site.

Lawns & Hydroseeding

Finn 1000-gallon hydroseeder coverage for large lawns, fields, and slopes — fast germination, even stands.

Green Roof Construction

Layered build-ups, drainage, growing medium, and plant install for green roofs that perform through the wet season.
