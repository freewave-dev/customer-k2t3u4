Services

A full landscape construction trade, in one company.

Every project is unique. The list below is a starting point — depending on what your project calls for, we pull resources from other fields and partner with other talented businesses across western Washington.

01

Commercial Prevailing Wage Projects

Public works with the certified payroll, documentation, and field discipline they require. Schools, municipalities, and agencies.

02

Irrigation Installation

Designed and installed irrigation for athletic fields, commercial sites, and large landscapes — heads, mains, controllers, all of it.

03

Earthwork & Grading

Laser-controlled dozer and hydraulic backhoe for rough cut through finish grade. Drainage, swales, building pads, sub-base.

04

Plant Selection & Installation

Material drawn from our own 18-acre Skagit Valley nursery. Trees, shrubs, ground covers — chosen for the site and installed by hand.

05

Lawns & Hydroseeding

Finn 1000-gallon hydroseeder for large lawn install, athletic fields, and erosion control on graded slopes.

06

Green Roof Construction

Layered build-ups — waterproofing protection, drainage, growing medium, plant install — engineered for the Pacific Northwest wet season.

Need something not listed? Tell us about the project. We'll either handle it in-house or bring in a trusted subcontractor we've worked with before.

Site work

From rough cut to finished landscape.

Most landscape construction problems start as grading problems. We start from the bottom — building pads, drainage, sub-base — and work up. Laser-controlled blade keeps tolerances tight; owned equipment keeps the schedule honest.

  • Rough & finish grading
  • Drainage & trenching
  • Sub-base preparation
  • Soils, amendments, topsoil
Plant install

Material from our own nursery.

Eighteen acres of growing ground a short drive from most of our jobsites means we choose plant material we know — trees, shrubs, and ground covers selected for the site, hardened off, and delivered when the project is ready for them.

  • Specimen trees & shrubs
  • Ground covers & perennials
  • Mulches & soil amendments
  • Maintenance & establishment
Specialty

Athletic fields, parks & green roofs.

Some categories require their own playbook. Athletic fields demand laser-flat sub-grade and consistent germination. Parks demand durability against constant public use. Green roofs demand engineered drainage and the right growing medium.

  • Sports field construction
  • Municipal & park projects
  • Vegetated & green roofs
  • Hydroseed & erosion control
